The Instagram page showcased a clear visual aesthetic while not compromising the functionality of each post.

Created with the grid structure of Instagram in mind, each post either displayed a member of the coalition, highlighting information about them and their campaign platform, or presented a tenet that the coalition stands for with further information in the caption.
